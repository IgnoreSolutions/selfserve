VSAT ResQ aims to be your preferred agent for VSAT antenna/satellite dish installation and service in Government and Commercial sectors.

We specialize in establishing communications for remote site applications as well as re-establishing communications after catastrophic events and weather.

We can provide you with Same Day Response for service outages.

We are field service technicians experienced with many types of communication systems:

  • Install and service your Very Small Aperture Terminal.
  • Install or repair your entire network of Category 5 or 6 cabling.
  • C-Band, SMAT, CCTV, TVRO, and air facility ground electronics.
  • Piloting and testing new equipment and networks.
